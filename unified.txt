Ibtikar

AI-powered mobile app that identifies harmful content in your Twitter/X feed.


Ibtikar is a mobile application designed to help users identify and filter potentially harmful content in their Twitter/X social media feeds. The app uses advanced AI technology, specifically a custom fine-tuned Arabic BERT (AraBERT) model, to analyze posts and tweets in real-time.
Key Features:
AI-Powered Content Analysis: Automatically scans posts from accounts you follow on Twitter/X and classifies them as "harmful", "safe", or "unknown" using machine learning models
Real-Time Safety Alerts: Provides immediate notifications about potentially harmful content in your feed
Privacy-Focused: Uses secure OAuth 2.0 authentication, encrypts sensitive data, and gives you full control over your information
User-Friendly Interface: Simple, intuitive design that makes it easy to understand content classifications
Data Transparency: Clear privacy policy and easy account deletion process
How It Works:
Connect your Twitter/X account securely via OAuth 2.0
The app analyzes posts from accounts you follow
AI models classify content and provide safety ratings
You receive alerts and insights about potentially harmful content
Privacy & Security:
All OAuth tokens are encrypted using Fernet encryption
Data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time
Full compliance with GDPR and CCPA regulations
